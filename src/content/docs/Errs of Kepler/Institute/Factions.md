## Brief history of faction B

Faction B did not originate cleanly. 

Faction B is born as a "rebel group" to contrast the general council's (Faction A at the time) stricter scheduling and demands. Something to contrast the general council's need for stifling order and procedure.

Established 10 years ago by a student named Vincenzo de Luca along with his friend (his "consigliere") Mera Montgomery. It started off as a small tutor group on the "darker" parts of magic as a whole, which the two are familiar about.

This eventually expanded into its own "underground" student group. The mafia-inspired structure is established by Vincenzo as an homage to his origins ~~(frankly, it's the only structure he knows)~~. After Vincenzo dropped out in his third year, Mera took over as the "Capo famiglia."

Over the years, rumors spread, establishing the presence of Faction B within the school's ecosystem. *It's important to note that there was not a fight for power. There was no "need" to clean the faction out from the school.*

***Faction B was permitted to exist.***
