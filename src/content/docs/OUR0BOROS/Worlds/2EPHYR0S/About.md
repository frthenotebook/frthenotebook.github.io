# Preface

What does it mean to be an entity of the $N$th dimension? What does it mean to be constantly rewritten by the environment that allows you to be? What does it mean to truly **know**?

An epistemology-driven world wherein knowing is simply defined as being able yo conceive and comprehend the truth, explanation non-withstanding. But then again, that isn't the definition we're used to, no?

`[2]EPHYR[0]S`, or `Zeph` as it is sometimes referred to, is the sole "sentient" entity of this iteration's $N$th dimension. This iteration is my attempt at exploring how the entity generally behaves in the $N$th dimension, what happens to its existence and the like. It also reveals the potential existence of a higher dimensional entity beyond n, which is mathematically true anyway.

On the other side, there is `[2]EPHYR[0]S`'s attempts at mimicking human motion that is to be applauded, even if it looks so bloody wrong in all sense of logic.

# About the Entity

## Introduction

`[2]EPHYR[0]S` doesn't belong to any storyline, nor is he restricted to just one universe. As such, they can be pictured interacting with anyone (with caveats).

his tragedy lies in its lonely existence, just like every even-numbered iteration within the cycle of creation and death.

she has no set identity; they have no set goals, nor wants. human constructs have no bearing on an entity far removed from humanity. it has no intelligence. it knows, it reacts, it moves, but it does not think. yet it knows more than any other entity in its realm.

it is not alive. it knows whatever the gut instinct tells, but it will never come to know why. it does not care for the why. it is not sentient.

**IT IS BENEATH HUMANITY. IT DOES NOT CARE.**

## Factoids

### The 'Abstract' Form versus the True Form

both forms cannot easily be perceived without consequences, for both forms are not of our realm.

what makes the true form different is that it is inconsistent. it changes at every millisecond. the chaos that the true form carries will bring forth worse effects to those trying to perceive it directly.

as such, the 'abstract' form is created as an 'in-between'. much more solid, much less chaos, and much more grounded. its existence is born from `[2]EPHYR[0]S`'s efforts to interact with lower dimensions in its higher-dimensional state.

the 'abstract' form tends to show itself when seeing the true form in a screen, or after long periods of staring at the true form.

### The Human Form

the human form is a byproduct of shifting into a form more befitting of the 3rd dimension.

like other entities, `[2]EPHYR[0]S` 'sees' other objects in 'sparklines'--thread-like objects that represent an object's shape. as it descends into the 3rd dimension, to fully assimilate into this realm, it sheds a fraction of itself in the shape of the average human. it is in control of how it behaves.

it moves and mimes, but never does it express. it doesn't breathe; it doesn't eat. it lives but it doesn't.

it mimics human speech by manipulating the sound waves in a specific way. it speaks gibberish, for it knows not what they mean. it mimics bodily movement by translating each part in accordance with a random human in vicinity. it expresses the wrong thing for it does not understand context.

it knows to mimic. it knows to move. but never rightly. and it does not care.
